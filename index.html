Cactus Plant Flea Market

x McDonald's

by Hallie Young

The collaboration between McDonald's and Cactus Plant Flea Market (CPFM) involves a limited-edition meal box that features a redesigned box and a choice of a Big Mac or 10-piece Chicken McNuggets, World Famous Fries and a drink. The box also includes one of four collectible figurines – Grimace, the Hamburglar, Birdie and Cactus Buddy. The collaboration also includes a range of merchandise for sale online only while supplies last.

The Cactus Plant Flea Market (CPFM) box collaboration with McDonald's features four collectible figurines – Grimace, the Hamburglar, Birdie, and Cactus Buddy – each of which has "two sets of eyes" and was designed by CPFM.

Cactus Buddy is a character created by CPFM, and it features prominently on the brand's clothing and accessories.

Grimace is a purple character with a wide smile and is one of the signature McDonald's mascots.

Birdie is a yellow bird who used to be a McDonald's mascot in the 1970s and 1980s

The Hamburglar is a mischievous character who is known for stealing hamburgers.

The collaboration between McDonald's and CPFM aims to appeal to younger consumers who are interested in streetwear culture and limited-edition merchandise. The partnership is part of McDonald's strategy to stay relevant and appeal to a new generation of consumers.

The Cactus Plant Flea Market (CPFM) toys included with the McDonald's collaboration have become highly sought-after items in the resale market. According to reports, the set of adult Happy Meal toys is being resold for thousands, with some listings reaching as high as $300,000.95 on eBay for a set of unopened products. The individual Cactus Buddy figure is being sold for as much as $35,999 on Grailed. Other figures such as Birdie, Hamburglar, and Grimace are going for between $20 and $40. The highest price paid for a set of sealed toys is $2,400. The resale market for the CPFM toys indicates the strong demand for limited-edition merchandise, especially among streetwear enthusiasts. The collaboration between McDonald's and CPFM has generated significant hype and interest, with fans willing to pay high prices for the exclusive items.

The collaboration between McDonald's and Cactus Plant Flea Market (CPFM) also includes a range of apparel featuring co-branded prints and graphics featuring characters from McDonald's, Big Mac imagery, coffee stirrers, and the famous golden arches sign. The capsule features hoodies, tees, and Hamburglar costumes, among other items. The merchandise collection is available on the Cactus Plant Flea Market website and through GOAT, a sneaker and streetwear marketplace. However, many of the items are currently sold out, including the Cactus Buddy and Friends tie-dye tee and the All Beef Hoodie, which were listed at $70. The apparel collection is part of McDonald's strategy to appeal to younger consumers who are interested in streetwear culture and limited-edition merchandise.
